All about the Amazon rainforest - the biggest rainforest in the world

Amazon rainforest is located in Brazil, Peru, Colombia, Ecuador and other South American countries

More than 30 million people of 350 diffrent ethnic groups live in the Amazon

Amazon is subdivided into 9 different national political systems and 3,344 formally acknowledged indigenous territories. Indigenous peoples make up 9% of the total population with 60 of the groups remaining largely isolated.
